Amazing Space Facts

Sun

The Sun holds 99.86% of the mass in our Solar System and is 109 times wider than Earth.

Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet and its extreme temperature swings are wild — from -173°C to 427°C!

Venus

Venus rotates backward and has a surface hot enough to melt lead. It's the hottest planet.

Earth

Earth is the only known planet with life and has liquid water on its surface.

Mars

Mars has the largest volcano in the solar system — Olympus Mons, which is over 22 km high.

Jupiter

Jupiter has 79 moons and the Great Red Spot — a massive storm raging for centuries.

Saturn

Famous for its beautiful rings made of ice and rock — stretching over 175,000 miles wide!

Uranus

Uranus spins on its side, making its seasons extreme and unlike any other planet.

Neptune

Neptune has supersonic winds — the fastest in the solar system, reaching 2,100 km/h!
