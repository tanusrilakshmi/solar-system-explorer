Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet and closest to the Sun. It has extreme temperature swings and no atmosphere.

Venus

Venus has a thick, toxic atmosphere and is the hottest planet due to its greenhouse effect.

Earth

Earth is the only known planet to support life, with abundant water and a breathable atmosphere.

Mars

Known as the Red Planet, Mars has the tallest volcano in the solar system and signs of ancient water.

Jupiter

The largest planet with over 75 moons and a massive storm called the Great Red Spot.

Saturn

Saturn is famous for its stunning rings made of ice and rock. It has over 80 moons.

Uranus

Uranus rotates on its side and has a pale blue color due to methane in its atmosphere.

Neptune

Neptune is a windy blue planet, known for its strong storms and deep blue color.

Pluto

Pluto is a dwarf planet in the Kuiper Belt. It has a heart-shaped glacier and thin atmosphere.
