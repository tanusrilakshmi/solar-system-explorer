Frequently Asked Questions

What is the solar system?

The solar system includes the Sun and all celestial objects that orbit around it such as planets, moons, asteroids, and comets.

Which planet is closest to the Sun?

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun.

Why is Venus hotter than Mercury?

Despite being farther from the Sun, Venus is hotter due to its dense atmosphere that traps heat through the greenhouse effect.

Is there water on Mars?

There is evidence of frozen water and possibly salty liquid water under the surface of Mars.

How many moons does Jupiter have?

Jupiter has over 90 known moons, including the four large Galilean moons: Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto.
